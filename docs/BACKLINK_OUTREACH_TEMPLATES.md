# Backlink Outreach Templates

```markdown
Adds `awesome-music-platforms`, a curated directory of platforms for independent artists, producers, beatmakers, sample-pack creators, labels, and music-tech builders.

It covers streaming, music distribution, beat selling, sample-pack marketplaces, sync licensing, promotion tools, analytics, creator storefronts, music visualization, and open-source music tools.

Repo: https://github.com/GareBear99/awesome-music-platforms
Hub: https://github.com/GareBear99/awesome-audio-lists

## Why it fits

This is not a general music-listening list. It maps practical release, monetization, promotion, licensing, and creator-tool surfaces for independent music workflows.
```
