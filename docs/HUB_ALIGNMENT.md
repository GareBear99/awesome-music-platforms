# Hub Alignment

`awesome-music-platforms` is the canonical music-platform satellite for the GareBear99 audio discovery network.

## Parent hub

- <https://github.com/GareBear99/awesome-audio-lists>

## Satellite role

This repo covers public music-platform discovery surfaces:

- streaming and listening platforms
- independent artist platforms
- music distribution
- sample-pack and loop marketplaces
- beat-selling platforms
- sync licensing and royalty-free music marketplaces
- promotion and discovery tools
- creator storefronts
- artist analytics
- music communities
- visualization tools
- open-source music projects
