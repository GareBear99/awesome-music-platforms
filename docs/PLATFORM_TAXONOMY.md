# Platform Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to keep submissions consistent and searchable.

## Core categories

| Category | Definition | Example use case |
|---|---|---|
| Streaming & Listening | Public music listening platforms and streaming services | Build audience and playlist discovery |
| Independent Artist Platforms | Artist-friendly upload/profile platforms | Drop WIPs, demos, remixes, albums, singles |
| Music Distribution | Services that deliver music to DSPs | Release to Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, Meta, YouTube |
| Sample Pack & Loop Marketplaces | Places to sell or discover loops, one-shots, presets, stems | Producer packs and royalty-free sounds |
| Beat Selling Platforms | Beat licensing and producer storefronts | Lease/exclusive beat sales |
| Music Licensing & Sync | Licensing marketplaces and sync platforms | Video, games, ads, creator content |
| Promotion & Discovery | Playlist, blog, curator, PR, and smart-link tools | Campaigns, pre-saves, fan gates |
| Creator Storefronts | Direct digital product/member/merch stores | Albums, stems, packs, subscriptions |
| Music Video & Short-Form | Video and short-form discovery platforms | Music videos, Shorts, Reels, TikTok clips |
| Analytics & Artist Tools | Analytics, dashboards, link tracking, campaign intelligence | Growth tracking and planning |
| Music Communities | Forums, communities, and discussion spaces | Feedback, collaboration, visibility |
| Audio Plugin & Producer Resources | Plugin directories, dev resources, producer tools | Audio-plugin and production ecosystem discovery |
| Music Visualization Tools | Reactive visuals, visualizers, and VJ tools | Visualizers, music videos, live shows |
| Open Source Music Projects | Open-source music/audio tools | DAWs, synths, visualization engines, audio tools |

## Submission quality bar

A platform should be included if it is public, useful, reasonably stable, and relevant to at least one real music workflow. Avoid referral links, thin clones, suspicious services, or pages that are mostly ads.
