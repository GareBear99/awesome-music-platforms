# Best Artist Storefronts

A guide to direct fan storefronts for music, merch, sample packs, stems, presets, memberships, and supporter-only drops.

## Platform comparison

| Platform | Best for |
|---|---|
| Bandcamp | Albums, tracks, merch, collector/fan culture |
| Gumroad | Digital products, packs, stems, templates |
| Payhip | Digital products and memberships |
| Shopify | Full custom commerce stack |
| Patreon | Recurring supporter content |
| Ko-fi | Donations, memberships, small shop |
| Buy Me a Coffee | Simple supporter memberships and tips |

## Best strategy

Use one canonical artist site or hub, then point every platform back to it. Your hub should explain where fans can listen free, buy downloads, support monthly, and access exclusive packs.
