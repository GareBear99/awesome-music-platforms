# Best Music Distribution Platforms

A practical guide for independent artists comparing distribution services for getting music onto Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, TikTok/Meta surfaces, and other DSPs.

## What to compare

| Factor | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Pricing model | Annual fees, per-release fees, free tiers, and revenue share can change the long-term cost. |
| Royalty handling | Some services keep no cut; others trade lower upfront cost for a revenue share. |
| Update speed | Fast metadata fixes, takedowns, and replacement audio uploads matter after release. |
| Publishing/admin | Publishing collection, Content ID, YouTube monetization, and neighboring rights can matter as catalog grows. |
| Label services | Marketing, playlist pitching, analytics, and team tools may matter for larger catalogs. |

## Independent artist recommendation

Start with one distributor that has a clean update/takedown workflow, then keep your canonical release links, smart links, UPC/ISRC metadata, artwork, and lyrics organized in your own hub.
