# Best Music Platforms for Independent Artists

Independent artists need more than one upload destination. A serious release stack usually includes:

1. A distribution platform for major DSP delivery.
2. A direct storefront for fan revenue.
3. A discovery platform for demos, WIPs, and organic reach.
4. A promotion stack for smart links, playlist/blog submissions, and campaigns.
5. Analytics tools to see what is actually working.

## Recommended starter stack

| Layer | Platforms to compare | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Distribution | DistroKid, TuneCore, CD Baby, RouteNote, LANDR | Sends music to major streaming platforms |
| Direct sales | Bandcamp, Gumroad, Payhip, Shopify | Keeps a direct fan-commerce layer |
| Discovery | SoundCloud, YouTube, TikTok, Audiomack | Builds audience and social proof |
| Promotion | SubmitHub, Groover, Feature.fm, Linkfire | Routes listeners and curators to releases |
| Analytics | Spotify for Artists, Apple Music for Artists, Chartmetric, Soundcharts | Tracks growth and campaign signals |
