# Best Music Promotion Tools

A guide to platforms used for playlist submissions, blog outreach, smart links, fan gates, pre-saves, and campaign analytics.

## Promotion categories

| Category | Tools to research |
|---|---|
| Curator/blog submissions | SubmitHub, Groover, Musosoup |
| Playlist campaigns | PlaylistPush, Daily Playlists |
| Smart links/pre-saves | Feature.fm, Linkfire, ToneDen, Hypeddit |
| Short-form funnel | TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts |
| Community traction | Reddit, Discord, Facebook Groups, SoundCloud repost networks |

## Rule of thumb

Promotion works best when the track has strong cover art, a short hook clip, clear genre positioning, a release link, and a call to action.
