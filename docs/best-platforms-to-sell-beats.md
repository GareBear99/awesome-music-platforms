# Best Platforms to Sell Beats

A focused guide for producers who want to sell beat leases, exclusive licenses, stems, and instrumental packs online.

## Best use cases

| Use case | Recommended platform type |
|---|---|
| Beat leases and exclusives | BeatStars, Airbit, Traktrain |
| Direct digital sales | Gumroad, Payhip, Shopify |
| Discovery funnel | YouTube, SoundCloud, TikTok, Instagram Reels |
| Membership access | Patreon, Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee |

## What to include with beats

- Clear license tiers
- MP3/WAV/stem options
- BPM and key
- Usage rights
- Producer tag policy
- Contact and custom-beat info

## Producer SEO angle

Each beat page should include genre, mood, BPM, key, similar-artist style terms where appropriate, and a direct purchase/license path.
