# Best Platforms to Sell Sample Packs

A guide for producers and sound designers selling loops, one-shots, MIDI, presets, stems, drum kits, construction kits, and sample libraries.

## Marketplace vs direct store

| Path | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| Marketplace | Built-in discovery and buyer trust | More competition, approval rules, lower control |
| Direct store | Brand ownership and flexible bundles | You must bring traffic yourself |
| Hybrid | Marketplace reach plus own canonical hub | More maintenance |

## Platforms to research

- Splice Sounds
- Loopmasters
- ADSR Sounds
- Producer Loops
- Loopcloud
- Gumroad
- Payhip
- Shopify
- Ko-fi shop

## Pack checklist

Include BPM, key, loop length, file format, sample rate, license, preview audio, cover art, demo track, and folder organization.
