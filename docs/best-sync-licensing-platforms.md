# Best Sync Licensing Platforms

A guide for artists and producers who want music placed in videos, games, ads, films, trailers, podcasts, and creator content.

## Platforms to research

- Songtradr
- Musicbed
- Artlist
- Epidemic Sound
- Pond5
- AudioJungle
- Marmoset
- PremiumBeat

## Submission preparation

- Clean metadata
- Instrumental versions
- Alternate lengths: 15s, 30s, 60s
- Stems if available
- Ownership/split documentation
- Mood and genre tags
- No uncleared samples

## Best-fit music types

Instrumentals, cinematic cues, electronic beds, ambient textures, trailer hits, lo-fi loops, corporate-friendly tracks, and game-ready loops can all work when tagged clearly.
