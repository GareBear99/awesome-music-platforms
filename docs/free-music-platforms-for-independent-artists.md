# Free Music Platforms for Independent Artists

A guide to free or low-cost places independent artists can upload, share, promote, or organize music online.

## Free discovery and hosting surfaces

- SoundCloud
- Audiomack
- YouTube
- Newgrounds Audio Portal
- HearThis.at
- Bandcamp free/listener-supported pages
- Reddit communities
- TikTok / Instagram / Facebook / YouTube Shorts for clips

## Use them as funnels

Free platforms are strongest when they point listeners toward your canonical hub, mailing list, storefront, distributor links, or supporter page.

## Suggested free stack

SoundCloud for WIPs and playlists, YouTube for visualizers, Bandcamp for direct support, GitHub for open music tools/lists, and a personal hub for everything else.
