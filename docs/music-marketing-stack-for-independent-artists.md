# Music Marketing Stack for Independent Artists

A practical music-marketing stack should connect release links, short-form content, direct fan capture, and analytics.

## Stack layers

| Layer | Purpose | Tools to compare |
|---|---|---|
| Smart link | One landing page for all stores | Feature.fm, Linkfire, ToneDen, Hypeddit |
| Playlist/blog submissions | Curator outreach | SubmitHub, Groover, Daily Playlists, Musosoup |
| Short-form funnel | Clips and visual hooks | TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook |
| Direct support | Fan revenue | Bandcamp, Patreon, Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee |
| Analytics | Measure real movement | Spotify for Artists, Apple Music for Artists, Soundcharts, Chartmetric |

## Rule

Do not rely on one platform. Use discovery platforms to send people toward owned or semi-owned surfaces: email list, storefront, hub site, Bandcamp, Patreon, or your own domain.
